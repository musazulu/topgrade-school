School Uniforms

Our students wear uniforms that reflect pride, discipline, and school spirit.

Sports Uniform

Comfortable blue tracksuit with school emblem — perfect for PE and outdoor activities.

Formal Uniform

Navy blazer, khaki shorts, striped tie, and wide-brimmed hat — worn for assemblies and special events.

Classroom Uniform

All students proudly wear their uniforms daily — fostering unity and focus in learning.

Uniform Guidelines
  • All uniforms must be clean and well-maintained.
  • Shoes should be black or white, polished and laced properly.
  • Hats are mandatory during outdoor activities and assemblies.
