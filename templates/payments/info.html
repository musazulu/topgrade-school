Payment Instructions

For Bank Transfers:
  1. Use student name as reference
  2. Email transfer proof to accounts@topgrade.edu
  3. Keep your transaction ID for verification
  4. Allow 24-48 hours for processing
For Cash Payments:
  • Pay at the school office between 8 AM - 3 PM
  • Bring student admission number
  • Request official receipt
  • No payments accepted after 3 PM
Important Notice

Late payments incur a 10% penalty fee. Students with outstanding fees may not be allowed to attend classes.
