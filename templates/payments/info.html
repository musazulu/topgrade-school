Enrollment Process

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Children aged 5-12 years are eligible for Grades 1-7.

Step 2: Submit Application

Fill out our online application form with all required information.

Apply Online Now
Step 3: Interview & Assessment

Schedule a meeting with our teachers for assessment.

Step 4: Acceptance & Payment

Complete fee payment to secure your child's place.

Required Documents:

  • Birth certificate (certified copy)
  • Previous school report (if applicable)
  • Immunization records
  • Parent/Guardian ID copy
  • Passport-sized photos (2 copies)
Important Notes
  • Applications are processed within 3-5 business days
  • Early enrollment is recommended as spaces are limited
  • For inquiries, contact: admissions@topgrade.edu
